# History

The school was founded in March 1992 at the initiative of E.I. Hurvits, who was then the head of the Zhovtnevyi district of Odesa and rightly believed that the unique building at 3 Sabaneyev Bridge, with its beauty, architecture, and history, should belong to the young generation of Odesa residents, who together with their teachers would turn it into a real Temple of Art.

Thus, the Center for Aesthetic Education of Children and Youth appeared under the direction of Alla Yevheniivna Pivnyak, and the first steps were taken on the difficult path of developing children's creativity.

The glorious traditions of theatrical Odesa and the lack of theater-profile educational institutions in the city prompted the school's teachers to direct the development of the institution in this direction.

So, in December 1996, under director Vladyslava Mykolaivna Sosytska, a new 'Art School No. 1 of theatrical profile' appeared, which retained its pop and art departments. This made it possible, along with theatrical projects, to open exhibitions of young artists and famous masters in the 'Mist' art gallery (headed by T.S. Mykhailychenko) and the super-popular TV show and festival 'Zirochka' (artistic director – O.S. Stepanov).

A new round of creative history for this institution came in 2003, which brought the building on Sabaneyev Bridge a new status and a new name: 'Children's Theatre School of Odesa.'

Since 2005, with the arrival of the new director Olena Borysivna Tkach, DTSH has been actively developing new projects – cooperation with the National Union of Theatre Workers, the Ukrainian Theatre named after V. Vasylko, the Odesa Puppet Theatre, the Municipal Museum of Personal Collections named after O.V. Bleschunov, the Odesa branch of the 'Intellectual Forum of Ukraine,' and other creative organizations.

In 2011, a very important event for all of us took place – DTSH moved to a new address – 24 Sofiivska St. Thanks to the efforts of caring people, the school solved its most painful issue – 'housing.' We are confident that in the new building, DTSH will reach new creative heights!

In 2020, on the eve of its 25th anniversary, 'Teatralo4ka' received a new status and name – Municipal Institution of Primary Specialized Education 'Art Theatre School of Odesa.'

Since 2009, with the support of the State Methodological Center at the Ministry of Culture of Ukraine, our school has been holding All-Ukrainian theatre seminars and workshops for teachers.

Since 2018, 'Teatralo4ka' has been holding these seminars at the end of the theatre season and at the beginning of the admission campaign – in June. In response to such a creative request, a unique theatre project appeared – the open festival of applicants to creative educational institutions 'Odesa.Theatre.PRO'.

Over these 3 festival years, ATSH Odesa has hosted representatives from 18 Ukrainian cities. Of the 72 participants, 44 became students of creative educational institutions.

All these years the school has been proud of its wonderful teachers who have worked since its foundation – true professionals: **S.N. Ryska, S.V. Kyseliova, O.V. Prorok**.

The school is proud of its brilliant students who conquer the juries of festivals and contacts of all levels every year. Our children successfully perform on professional theatre stages and in films like 'Catch Kaidash', 'Butterfly Vision' (Cannes Film Festival), and others.

Our children imperceptibly turn into graduates, into successful people, who do not forget their alma mater – the Art Theatre School.

A wonderful tradition of our school has become the **'Training Theatre'**, which unites graduates of different years and is a creative platform for students.

We can proudly state that the Art Theatre School has become a truly professional educational institution and has firmly taken its place as a basic school of primary theatre education in Ukraine!
