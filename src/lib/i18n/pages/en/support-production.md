# ДТШ-production

**ДТШ-production** is the heart of our digital creativity. We strive to capture and preserve every important moment of our school: every performance, every note, and every student's smiling glance.

However, modern content production is a complex and costly process. To ensure that the school's history remains accessible and is preserved in proper quality, we need to constantly support the operations of our sound and video studio. Filming, securely storing terabytes of materials, editing, and updating professional equipment all require significant resources.

Your support helps us address the following needs:
*   **Cloud Storage:** paying for services to securely archive a huge collection of performance photos and videos.
*   **Technical Maintenance:** repairing and maintaining video cameras, photo cameras, and computer hardware for editing.
*   **Equipment Updates:** purchasing modern stage lighting and sound equipment for high-quality recording of school events.

It's not just about technology — it's about preserving our school's living history. We would be grateful for any support in developing our media studio.
