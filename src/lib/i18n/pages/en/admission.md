# Enrollment at Odesa Theatre School for the 2025-2026 academic year

## Theatre Department (elementary level)
**Target audience:** students of grades 1-3 of general education school (on a self-financing basis)
**Subjects:** acting, stage speech, dance, noise orchestra, vocals, fairy tale therapy, visual arts.

## Theatre Department (basic level)
**Target audience:** students of grades 6-7 of general education school
**Subjects:** acting, stage speech, Ukrainian rituals, dance, stage movement, vocals, theatre history.

## Music Department
* **Piano class** — students of grades 1-2
* **Guitar class** — students of grade 5

## Documents required for admission:
1. Application of the established form (filled in on site)
2. Contract-agreement with parents (signed on site)
3. Copy of the child's birth certificate
4. Medical certificate from a doctor stating that there are no contraindications to additional loads.

**Document submission:** from June 2 to June 25, 2025
