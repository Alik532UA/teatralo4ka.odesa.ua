# Student Enrollment at Odesa Theatre School for the 2026-2027 academic year

## Theatre Department (elementary level)
**Target audience:** students of grades 2-4 of general education school (on a self-financing basis)  
**Subjects:** acting, stage speech, dance, noise orchestra, vocals, fairy tale therapy, visual arts.

## Theatre Department (basic level)
**Target audience:** students of grades 5-7 of general education school  
**Subjects:** acting, stage speech, Ukrainian rituals, dance, stage movement, vocals, theatre history.

## Theatre Department (intensive class)
**Target audience:** students of grades 8-9 of general education school (on a self-financing basis)  
**Subjects:** acting, stage speech, dance, stage movement, vocals, theatre history.

## Art Department
**Target audience:** students of grades 4-6 of general education school  
**Subjects:** drawing, composition, painting, introductory course in history of visual arts.

## Music Department
* **Choral singing class** — students of grade 1 of general education school  
  Subjects: choral singing, music theory, noise orchestra, dance, acting, fairy tale therapy
* **Piano class** — students of grades 1-2 of general education school
* **Solo singing class** — students of grade 5 of general education school
